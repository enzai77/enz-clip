Frequently Asked Questions

How does ENZ CLIP work?
Paste any YouTube URL, choose your clip style and duration. Our AI analyzes audio energy peaks to find the most engaging moments and automatically cuts them into short clips.
Is it really free?
Yes! The Starter plan is 100% free with 10 clips per month. Upgrade to Pro or Agency for more clips and features.
What video formats are supported?
We support all public YouTube videos. Clips are exported as MP4 files optimized for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts (9:16 vertical format).
How long does it take to generate clips?
Most videos are processed in 2-5 minutes depending on the video length and your internet speed.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your PayPal subscription anytime from your PayPal account. No hidden fees or cancellation charges.
Do clips have watermarks?
Free plan clips have a small ENZ CLIP watermark. Pro and Agency plans support custom branding with your own logo.
